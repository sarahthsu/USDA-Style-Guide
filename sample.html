Welcome to the USDA Guidelines!

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) is a government resource that educates Americans about the federal government’s role in regulating food, agriculture, natural resources, rural development, and nutrition within the United States of America. The USDA offers access to current information in a clear and engaging format while communicating in a personable and inclusive voice.

Typography

Brandon Grotesque

Brandon Grotesque has a functional look with a warm touch. The clean lines and restrained forms are professional, while the rounded curves are warm and personable.

Futura

Futura is a geometric sans-serif typeface that has an appearance of efficiency and forwardness.

Gill Sans

Gill Sans is a cleanly modern and classical at the same time.
